Frequently Asked Questions

How can I book a sports facility?

You can browse facilities on our homepage and click "Book Facility" to reserve a slot. You'll need to create an account first if you haven't already.

Can I cancel or reschedule a booking?

Yes, bookings can be cancelled or rescheduled up to 2 hours before the start time through your account dashboard. Late cancellations may be subject to fees.

Do I need to create an account?

Yes, an account is required to manage your bookings and access exclusive offers. Creating an account takes less than a minute and gives you access to all our features.

What payment options are available?

We support credit/debit cards, UPI, and net banking via our secure payment gateway. All transactions are encrypted for your security.

What are your operating hours?

Our facilities are open from 6:00 AM to 10:00 PM daily, including weekends and public holidays. Some facilities may have different hours during special events.
